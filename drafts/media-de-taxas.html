Média de taxas

Wilson Freitas

 02/11/2015
Categoria: finance

$$ [\Pi_i (1 + r_i)]^{1/12} $$

tomando o $\log$:

$$ \sum_i \frac{\log(1 + r_i)}{12} $$

$$ y_i = \log(1 + r_i) $$

$$ y_i = T_i + S_i + e_i $$

$$ \log(1 + r_i) = \log(1 + t_i) + \log(1 + s_i) + \log(1 + e_i) \ \log(1 + r_i) = \log[(1 + t_i)(1 + s_i)(1 + e_i)] \ (1 + r_i) = (1 + t_i)(1 + s_i)(1 + e_i) \ $$
